Stomata Counter

A tool to automatically estimate stomatal density from epidermal micrographs. Experimental web frontend. Running on my local machine; do not distribute.

Read this BEFORE imaging specimens

This method for detecting stomata requires high magnification images of epidermal surfaces that are in focus and relatively free of specimen preparation and anatomical features (e.g. trichomes) that distort the visual identification of stoma. If you can't decide if it's a stoma, the method may not either. You are responsbile for creating a collection of high quality, homogenous images. All input images must be taken at 400x. Color images do not help or hurt the method. To help you understand the distribution of error, you can annotate a set of images after uploading and automatically identifying stomata. If your images perform particularly poorly, annotate them for stomata and contact us. We may retrain the neural network using your images and annotations.
